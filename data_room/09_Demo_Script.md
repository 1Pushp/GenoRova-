# GenorovaAI - Demo Script

Date: April 17, 2026

## Demo Goal

Show GenorovaAI as a credible computational research-support product with an
authenticated workspace, ranked candidate review, and explicit scientific
limitations.

## 3-5 Minute Sequence

1. Open the login or signup screen.
2. State the framing:
   - "GenorovaAI is a prototype computational research-support platform."
   - "The strongest workflows today are scoring, explanation, comparison, ranking, and responsible trust messaging."
3. Sign in and pause on the guided workspace home state.
4. Run the ranked-candidate demo prompt.
5. Review the candidate card and validation ledger.
6. Ask for a plain-language explanation.
7. Show one follow-up optimization or comparison request.
8. Close with the limitation statement.

## Safe Prompt Sequence

Prompt 1:

`Show the top computational candidates in the active diabetes workflow`

Prompt 2:

`Explain the best molecule simply`

Prompt 3:

`Optimize the best molecule for oral delivery`

Prompt 4:

`Score this molecule: CCO`

## What To Say While The Ranked View Is Open

- "This is an evidence-weighted ranked candidate set, not experimental proof."
- "The validation ledger separates exact descriptor calculations from heuristic or proxy fields."
- "The platform is strongest today for ranking, explanation, comparison, and transparent caveat handling."

## Short Spoken Disclaimer

"This is a computational research-support platform. The visible outputs include
exact descriptor calculations plus heuristic and proxy signals. They are not
experimental proof or clinical validation."

## What To Emphasize

- protected user workspace
- guided onboarding and trust framing
- ranked candidate presentation
- validation ledger readability
- honest generation limits

## What Not To Say

Do not say:

- "GenorovaAI has validated a new drug"
- "These outputs prove efficacy"
- "This is clinically validated"
- "The model reliably generates new drug candidates today"
- "This is production-ready biotech software"

## If Generation Comes Up

Use this answer:

"Generation exists in the product, but it remains the weakest scientific area
right now. The honest position is scoring, explanation, comparison, ranked
review, and disciplined research-support workflows while generation quality
continues to improve."

## Best Closing Line

"The value of GenorovaAI today is disciplined computational support and
responsible product design around scientific uncertainty."
