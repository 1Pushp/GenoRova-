# GenorovaAI - Deck Talking Points

Date: April 17, 2026

Use these points to keep any pitch deck, faculty packet, or external summary
aligned to the current honest product state.

## Core Positioning

- GenorovaAI is a prototype computational research-support platform.
- The strongest workflows today are ranked candidate review, known-molecule scoring, explanation, comparison, and protected workspace guidance.
- Outputs include exact descriptor calculations plus heuristic and proxy signals.
- Outputs are not experimental proof, clinical validation, or evidence of efficacy.

## Strongest Product Claims

- authenticated workspace with guided onboarding
- evidence-weighted ranked candidate presentation
- structured validation ledger with caveats
- conservative handling of weak generation
- real working full-stack product path with smoke checks and runtime status

## Conservative Science Claims

- active product story is diabetes / DPP4 / sitagliptin comparator
- ranking is comparator-aware and evidence-weighted
- generation remains the weakest scientific area
- fallback outputs should be described as fallback outputs, not fresh discoveries

## What To Avoid In External Materials

- "validated drug discovery engine"
- "novel lead discovery platform" without qualification
- "production-ready biotech software"
- "experimentally confirmed pipeline"
- "reliably generates new drug molecules"

## Safe One-Sentence Description

"GenorovaAI is a prototype computational research-support platform that helps
users rank, score, explain, and compare candidate molecules inside an
authenticated workspace while keeping scientific limitations explicit."

## Safe Closing Line

"The current value is disciplined computational support and responsible product
design around scientific uncertainty, not proof of a validated therapeutic
discovery engine."
